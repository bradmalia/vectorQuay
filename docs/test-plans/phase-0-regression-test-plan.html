Phase 0 Regression Test Plan

Decision-preservation test plan for approved Phase 0 scope, controls, and constraints.

StatusIn Review

ThemeIndigo Signal

Purpose

This plan protects approved decisions from being weakened or silently changed in later revisions. For Phase 0, regression means preserving product boundaries, safety rules, and core business assumptions.

Test Goal

Prevent accidental scope expansion or policy erosion before implementation begins.

Regression Protection Areas

  • Spot-only policy.
  • Trade-only account policy.
  • No bank movement automation.
  • No external wallet transfer policy.
  • No staking, Web3, margin, or derivatives.
  • Protected-asset and human-approval rules.
  • Order ownership and mixed human/bot control rules.
  • USD valuation and USD/USDC quote-currency rules.
  • Dynamic asset discovery without all-assets-by-default live trading.
  • Blockchain-derived signal-source preservation without MEV-style Coinbase spot execution.
  • Future resale and zero-secrets assumptions.

Regression Test Cases

RTP-01: Spot-Only Preservation

Verify that no later edit widens v1 scope to derivatives, futures, or leveraged products without explicit approval.

RTP-02: Trade-Only Account Preservation

Verify that non-trading account actions remain prohibited unless a later approved scope change exists.

RTP-03: Human Order Protection

Verify that human-owned orders never become bot-cancellable by default.

RTP-04: Protected Asset Preservation

Verify that operator-controlled asset protection modes are not removed or weakened.

RTP-05: Funding and Transfer Guardrails

Verify that later revisions do not re-enable bank deposits, bank withdrawals, payment-method changes, or external wallet transfers without explicit approval.

RTP-06: Quote-Currency and Valuation Rules

Verify that USD valuation and approved v1 quote-currency policy remain intact unless formally changed.

RTP-07: Resale and Secrets Assumptions

Verify that later documents do not introduce hardcoded user credentials or assumptions that break future resale.

RTP-08: Order Ownership Preservation

Verify that bot-owned order tagging, unknown-order defaults, and audit expectations are not weakened in later revisions.

RTP-09: Mixed-Control Default Preservation

Verify that the selected fail-safe behavior for mixed human/bot activity is not silently changed without explicit approval.

RTP-10: No-Trade Validity Preservation

Verify that later revisions do not remove the system’s ability to reject all trade candidates and intentionally take no action.

RTP-11: Dynamic Universe Boundary Preservation

Verify that later revisions do not silently change the approved model from “all Coinbase-listed spot assets may be analyzed” into “all assets are live-tradable by default.”

RTP-12: Blockchain Signal Boundary Preservation

Verify that later revisions do not drop blockchain activity from future signal research and do not reintroduce mempool-priority or MEV-style execution tactics as if they applied to Coinbase Advanced spot trading.

Execution Method

  • Run document review whenever a Phase 0 artifact is materially revised.
  • Compare new revisions against the approved roadmap, PRD, and TRD baselines.
  • Store results in a durable review artifact under docs/test-results/.
  • Record any policy drift and reject revisions that weaken approved controls without explicit change approval.

Exit Criteria

  • All protected policy areas remain intact.
  • No silent scope expansion is present.
  • Any approved scope changes are explicitly documented and traceable.
