Phase 0 Integration Test Plan

Cross-document validation for roadmap, PRD, TRD, and Phase 0 decision outputs.

Versionv1.0
StatusIn Review
OwnerBrad Malia
ThemeIndigo Signal

Purpose

This plan verifies that the approved Phase 0 roadmap, PRD, and technical requirements operate as a coherent system of record. For Phase 0, integration quality means traceability, consistency, and absence of contradictory guidance.

Test Goal

Prove that the planning artifacts align well enough to support Phase 0 research without ambiguity or policy drift.

Scope

  • Validate consistency between roadmap v4, Phase 0 PRD, and Phase 0 technical requirements.
  • Validate traceability of business policy into technical policy.
  • Validate that approved constraints are preserved across documents.
  • Validate that required Phase 0 outputs are represented consistently.

Out of Scope

  • Executable software testing.
  • API integration testing.
  • GUI behavior testing.
  • Trading-strategy validation.

Integration Test Areas

  • Roadmap to PRD alignment.
  • PRD to TRD traceability.
  • Permissions policy consistency.
  • Asset-universe and quote-currency consistency.
  • Analysis-universe versus live-trading-universe consistency.
  • Human/bot mixed-control policy consistency.
  • Research method and evidence-standard consistency.
  • Future signal-source boundary consistency.
  • Security and secrets policy consistency.
  • Deliverable and exit-criteria consistency.

Test Cases

ITP-01: Roadmap Objective Alignment

Verify that the PRD and TRD reflect the roadmap’s objective of aggressive but controlled growth.

Pass condition: no document redefines the product objective in a conflicting way.

ITP-02: Spot-Only and Trade-Only Alignment

Verify that roadmap, PRD, and TRD all preserve Coinbase spot-only and trade-only behavior.

Pass condition: no document reintroduces staking, Web3, derivatives, bank flows, or external transfers.

ITP-03: Permissions Matrix Traceability

Verify that every major permission in the PRD has corresponding technical treatment in the TRD.

Pass condition: no approved policy is absent from technical enforcement planning.

ITP-04: Protected Asset Policy Consistency

Verify that protected-asset modes are defined consistently across business and technical documents.

Pass condition: `no buy`, `no sell`, and `fully excluded` are preserved without contradiction.

ITP-05: Mixed Human/Bot Control Consistency

Verify that order ownership, unknown-order handling, and human-activity policy are reflected consistently.

Pass condition: unknown orders default to human-owned and cancellation of human-owned orders remains approval-gated everywhere.

ITP-06: Tradable Universe Consistency

Verify that quote-currency restrictions, liquidity rules, and candidate asset outputs are required consistently.

Pass condition: PRD and TRD agree on USD/USDC policy and viability-threshold expectations.

ITP-07: Phase Output Consistency

Verify that PRD and TRD require the same class of Phase 0 deliverables and decision outputs.

Pass condition: no key output exists in one document and is missing from the other.

ITP-08: Research Method Consistency

Verify that the technical research method and evidence standard remain consistent with Phase 0 feasibility expectations.

Pass condition: research sources, confidence handling, and finding-record expectations are not contradicted elsewhere.

ITP-09: Security and Secrets Consistency

Verify that PRD and TRD align on zero-secrets-in-repo, bring-your-own-credentials, and configuration-separation expectations.

Pass condition: no document introduces conflicting secret-handling or deployment assumptions.

ITP-10: Research Findings Format Consistency

Verify that the planned Phase 0 findings artifact format matches the TRD requirement for question, conclusion, source, confidence, impact on v1, and unresolved assumptions.

Pass condition: the findings format preserves the required research schema without omission.

ITP-11: Dynamic Asset Discovery Consistency

Verify that roadmap, PRD, TRD, and findings all preserve the distinction between the broad analysis universe and the narrower live trading universe.

Pass condition: documents consistently state that all Coinbase-listed spot assets may be analyzed over time, while only a screened subset may become live-tradable.

ITP-12: Blockchain Signal Boundary Consistency

Verify that the artifacts consistently preserve blockchain-derived data as a future signal-source area while excluding mempool or MEV-style execution tactics from Coinbase Advanced spot trading.

Pass condition: no document implies that Coinbase spot execution should use miner-priority or validator-priority mechanics, and no document drops blockchain activity from future intelligence consideration.

Execution Method

  • Perform manual document review with line-by-line comparison across approved artifacts.
  • Record each integration finding as pass, fail, or needs clarification.
  • Store results in a durable review artifact under docs/test-results/.
  • Any inconsistency must be resolved in the source documents before Phase 0 research begins.

Exit Criteria

  • No unresolved contradictions remain across roadmap, PRD, and TRD.
  • All critical policies are traceable from business rule to technical requirement.
  • All Phase 0 decision outputs are represented consistently.
