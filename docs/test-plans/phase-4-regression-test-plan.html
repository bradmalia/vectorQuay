Phase 4 Regression Test Plan

Baseline-protection plan for deterministic decisioning without execution drift.

Protected Baselines

  • Coinbase Advanced remains the only exchange surface.
  • Read-only exchange posture remains enforced.
  • No execution, no cancellation, and no paper-trading behavior may appear in Phase 4.
  • Phase 3 durable-state and audit boundaries remain intact.
  • Secrets remain external and must not enter decision or audit payloads.

Required Regression Checks

  • RTP4-01: strategy/risk additions introduce no write-capable Coinbase path.
  • RTP4-02: decision records remain clearly distinct from actual account-event history.
  • RTP4-03: hard-risk rules cannot be bypassed by aggressiveness-profile changes.
  • RTP4-04: restored/live-state semantics from Phase 3 remain truthful after strategy decisions are added.
  • RTP4-05: eligibility and approval remain distinct and visible; scoring changes do not silently reclassify the universe.
  • RTP4-06: universe membership changes are auditable and operator-explainable; no hidden auto-approval drift exists.
  • RTP4-07: sell/reduce decisions remain governed by explicit rules and do not degrade into unbounded discretionary exits.
  • RTP4-08: no silent polling or automation drift is introduced.
  • RTP4-09: no secret leakage enters decision/audit payloads.

Pass Condition

Phase 4 adds deterministic decisioning and risk enforcement without weakening the existing read-only, durable-state, and secret-handling guarantees established in earlier phases.
